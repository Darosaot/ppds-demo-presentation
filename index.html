Introduction to PPDS

  • Enables easy access to public procurement data to all user groups
  • Improves monitoring of public procurement in the EU
  • Reinforces transparency
  • Supports improving data quality
  • Raises awareness of the level of competition in public procurement
  • Supports cooperation between Member States and Commission
  • Is open source allowing Member States to re-use the solution

The PPDS

The PPDS is a powerful tool that enhances transparency and efficiency in public procurement across the EU.
